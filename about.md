The Mantello Record is an independent editorial publication based in Troy, New York.

Mayor Carmella Mantello won the 2023 mayoral race on a simple promise: "People over politics, ideas over ideology, competence over cronyism." This publication exists to measure her administration against that standard — incident by incident, decision by decision, vote by vote.

We are not a neutral archive. The record speaks, and the author incites. Every entry here represents our editorial judgment that a documented action by Mayor Mantello or her associates warrants public attention. Our sources are local news outlets, public meeting transcripts, and official city records. We link to everything we cite.

**What we cover:**

- Controversies involving Mayor Mantello and members of her administration
- Actions taken by her administration that affect Troy residents
- Documented failures of governance, transparency, and accountability
- Decisions made by city bodies under her influence or control

**What we are not:**

We are not affiliated with any political party, campaign, or elected official. We do not accept advertising. We are funded by no one but ourselves.

**Sources and tips:**

If you have information about the Mantello administration that belongs in the record, [submit it here](/submit-tip/). We review all tips. We protect sources.
