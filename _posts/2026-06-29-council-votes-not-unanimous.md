## 2026

The seven-member Democratic council seated in January 2026 voted unanimously on nearly all matters. One vote departed from 7-0 in the first six months of the new term.

**March 19, 2026** — Committee Night and Finance Meeting

- **Res. 35** (Flock Safety ALPR contract renewal, $78,000): **Tabled 7-0.** The council voted unanimously to table the contract pending additional information. Mantello subsequently issued a state of emergency on April 1 to authorize the payment without a council vote, and the council sued her in Rensselaer County Supreme Court in May to have the order declared unlawful.

*Source: 2026-03-19_Committee_Night_Finance_Meeting.md*
